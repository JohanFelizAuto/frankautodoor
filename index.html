What We Do

Our Services

Comprehensive sliding door repair and service — from minor adjustments to full mechanism replacements.

⚙️

Motor Replacement

Power sliding door motor failure is the #1 issue. We replace worn or burned-out motors with genuine OEM units for reliable, long-lasting operation.

🔗

Cable & Track Repair

Frayed cables and damaged tracks cause doors to jam, stick, or derail. We repair or replace the full cable assembly and track system.

🔒

Latch & Lock Service

Broken door latches can prevent your sliding door from closing securely. We replace latch assemblies and lock actuators to restore full function.

🧠

Control Module Diagnostics

Electrical gremlins? We diagnose and repair sliding door control modules, wiring harnesses, and switches that control your door's operation.

🔄

Roller & Hinge Repair

Worn rollers and bent hinges cause grinding noises and rough door movement. We restore smooth, effortless sliding door operation.

🔍

Full Inspection & Tune-Up

Preventive maintenance to keep your sliding door working perfectly. We inspect, lubricate, adjust, and tighten all critical components.
